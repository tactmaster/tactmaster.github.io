Set oven to 180 degrees C.

![Baking Powder](/img/bakingpowder.jpeg ){:height="200px" }

Ingredients:s
* 45 g flour
* 30 g butter
* 15 g sugar

Method:
1. Mix together: flour, butter and sugar.
2. Roll out and cut out shapes.
3. Bake in oven for 15 minutes.
