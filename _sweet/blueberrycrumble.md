Ingredients:

| ![Oats](/img/oats.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Flour](/img/flour.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Butter](/img/butter.jpg){:height="100px"} |
| 2 dl oats | 1 dl flour | 50 g butter |
| ![Syrup](/img/syrup.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Cardamom](/img/cardamom.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Blueberries](/img/blueberries.jpg){:height="100px"} |
| 0.5 dl syrup | Cardamom (some!) | Blueberries (lots!) |

Method:

1. Fry oats, flour and cardamom in butter and syrup until nice and brown.
2. Empty frying pan and fry blueberries until soft.
3. Sprinkle crumble over the top of the blueberries and serve with squirty cream.
