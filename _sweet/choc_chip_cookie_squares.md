| 300 g butter | 1 dl sugar | 2 dl brown sugar |
| ![Egg](/img/egg.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Flour](/img/flour.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Bicarb](/img/bicarb.jpg){:height="100px"} |
| 1 egg | 6 dl flour | 1 teaspoon bicarb |
| ![Baking powder](/img/baking powder.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Vanilla](/img/vanilla.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Chocolate chips](/img/chocchips.jpg){:height="100px"} |
| 1 teaspoon baking powder | 1 teaspoon vanilla | 150 g dark chocolate chips |

Method:
1. Whisk together butter, sugar and brown sugar until light coloured.
2. Add egg and whisk some more.
3. Add flour, bicarb, baking powder and chocolate chips and mix some more.
4. Squish the dough into a 30 x 40 cm baking tin lined with baking paper.
5. Bake for around 15 minutes.
