Ingredients:

| ![Butter](/img/butter.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Milk](/img/milk.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Flour](/img/flour.jpg){:height="100px"} |
| 63 g melted butter | 125 ml milk | 120 g flour |
| ![Egg](/img/egg.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Baking powder](/img/bakingpowder.jpg){:height="100px"} | ![Water](/img/water.jpg){:height="100px"} |
| 0.5 egg | 0.5 teaspoon baking powder | 100 ml water |

Method:

1. Melt butter and allow it to cool.
2. Beat together milk, flour, egg and baking powder into a smooth batter.
3. Beat in melted butter and water.
4. Cook in waffle iron.
